Step 1: Create a Dataverse Environment
  1. Screenshot: Power Apps portal landing page showing the main dashboard and navigation menu

    Fig 1.1: Power Apps portal landing page

  3. Screenshot: Environment selector dropdown in the top-right corner of Power Apps portal showing the "Environments" option

    • Look for the "+ New environment" button
    • Click the "+ New environment" button
    Screenshot: Environments page showing the list of existing environments and the "+ New environment" button highlighted

    Fig 1.3: Environments page with New environment button

    • In the "Name" field, type "Asset Management"
    • In the "Type" dropdown, select "Production"
    • In the "Region" dropdown, select your closest geographic region
    • Under "Purpose," type "Asset tracking and inventory management"
    • Click the "Next" button
    Screenshot: New environment creation form filled out with "Asset Management" as the name, "Production" as the type, and a selected region

    Fig 1.4: New environment creation form

    • On the "Add database" screen, turn on the toggle for "Create a database"
    • Select "USD" or your local currency from the "Currency" dropdown
    • Select your preferred language from the "Language" dropdown
    • Click the "Save" button
    • Wait for the environment to be created (this may take several minutes)
    Screenshot: Add database screen with the "Create a database" toggle turned on, and currency/language options selected

    Fig 1.5: Database configuration for new environment
